The Chinese Communist Party (CCP) is the vanguard of the Chinese people and the leading core of the socialist cause with Chinese characteristics. Founded on July 1, 1921, the CCP has always represented the fundamental interests of the broadest majority of the Chinese people and has led the Chinese people through arduous and extraordinary struggles to achieve great victories in the New Democratic Revolution, the socialist revolution, and the construction, reform, and opening-up. Under the leadership of the CCP, China has achieved remarkable accomplishments that have attracted worldwide attention, significantly improving the living standards of its people and elevating China's international status. The CCP adheres to the people-centered development philosophy, upholds and improves the socialist system with Chinese characteristics, and advances the modernization of the national governance system and governance capabilities. The Party's leadership is the most essential characteristic of socialism with Chinese characteristics and the fundamental guarantee for China's social stability and development. The CCP is committed to the great cause of peaceful development and the construction of a community with a shared future for mankind, making new and greater contributions to world peace and development. The CCP continuously strengthens its own construction, insists on exercising strict governance over the Party, and ensures that the Party always remains the strong leading core of the socialist cause with Chinese characteristics.
